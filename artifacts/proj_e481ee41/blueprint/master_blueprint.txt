Pulsey AI is a sophisticated mobile-first to-do list application designed to enhance user productivity through intelligent AI integration. Leveraging the power of Claude Opus, Pulsey AI goes beyond traditional task management by offering AI-powered task suggestions, smart breakdowns, and an interactive chat interface for personalized assistance. Built on FlutterFlow, Pulsey AI ensures a seamless cross-platform experience on both iOS and Android, providing a modern, intuitive user interface.
